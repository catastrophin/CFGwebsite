The D-LYT Bookstore

The D-LYT bookstore is an award-winning children's literacy nonprofit. At D-LYT, we hold to one simple truth: Reading matters. It’s an early gateway skill that paves the pathway for future success. Without reading skills, kids are simply less likely to graduate high school, find employment, or successfully avoid other hardships like poverty, homelessness, hunger, and injustice.

Since 2018, D-LYT has been pairing caring adult volunteers with children to provide reading support and books to keep. Our volunteers read one-on-one or in small group with children, modeling a love of reading and building children’s reading skills and self-confidence in a positive, child-guided environment.
Our Vision is to make all children realize their full potential through reading.
Our Mission is to spark joy and opportunity through the magic of a shared book .

In 2019, we launched a brand new e-store because reading materials come in many different forms. We aim to be more accessible to the new age readers and keep it fun for everyone!
